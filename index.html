FAQ

What is a HEIC file?

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is the default photo format on iPhones since iOS 11. It produces smaller files than JPG while maintaining the same quality, but it's not widely supported on Windows, websites, and older devices. Converting HEIC to JPG ensures your photos work everywhere.
